## Hello, it's been a while...
Hi. It's been a bit over a year since I lasted posted. You know how it is, life gets busy. Lots of things have happened and changed since January 2025. The biggest event being moving back to America and starting a new job in October 2025. As I'm settling back into the land of the free and home of the brave, I find myself comparing almost everything about America to Japan. Also, I am working towards spending my time towards more fulfilling hobbies, so I wanted to write more and here we are.

Since I found myself too busy to keep up with this blog while I lived in Nagoya, but I still want to write about my time there, I want to start a series called Nagoya Nostalgia. This series will contain writings of my time living there in hindsight and from the best of my memory. Note that even though I'm calling it Nagoya Nostalgia doesn't mean everything I write about in this series happens in Nagoya. Rather, the events I am writing about happened during the time period of my life where I was living in Nagoya.

And with that, please look forward to this series :)
